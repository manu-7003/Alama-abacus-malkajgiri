About Alama Abacus

Alama International Abacus is dedicated to nurturing young minds through the ancient art of abacus calculation. With our specialized curriculum, we transform mathematical learning into an engaging journey that builds confidence and cognitive abilities.

Founded by experts with over 20 years of experience, our methodology combines traditional abacus techniques with modern educational approaches to deliver exceptional results.
